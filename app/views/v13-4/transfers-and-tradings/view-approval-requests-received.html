What do the transfer statuses mean?
Status What this means
TRANSFER REQUEST SENT The transfer request has been sent to the energy supplier but they have not accepted or declined it yet.
ASSESSING TRANSFER REQUEST The transfer request is being assessed for approval by Ofgem's internal team.
TRANSFER REQUEST WITHDRAWN The energy supplier has withdrawn the transfer request.
TRANSFER DECLINED The energy supplier declined your transfer request.
TRANSFER NOT APPROVED Ofgem did not approve the transfer request.
TRANSFER APPROVED Ofgem has approved the transfer request.
