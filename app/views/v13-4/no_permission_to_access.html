Accessing and using the Great British Insulation Scheme

Only the authorised signatory for your organisation can request Great British Insulation Scheme user profiles from Ofgem.

To request a user profile, you must complete and attach the 'authorised data provider statement of authorisation form'. Then, send this to by email at GBIS.enquiry@ofgem.gov.uk.

You can read more guidance on requesting Great British Insulation Scheme user profiles on the Ofgem website at Great British Insulation Scheme - The Great British Insulation Scheme portal | Ofgem.
