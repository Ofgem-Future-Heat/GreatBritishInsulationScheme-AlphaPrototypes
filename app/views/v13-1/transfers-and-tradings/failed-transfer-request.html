[GBIS_TR000060] measure transfer request not sent

Why was this transfer not sent?

Measures within the transfer request may not have met the correct criteria. Measures in the transfer request must:

  • exist in the GBIS register
  • be set to 'Approved' or 'Rejected'
  • not already be part of an existing transfer request
  • belong to your organisation

What to do next

To do this, you must:

  1. Download the error report.
  2. Follow the instructions in the error report to fix the errors in the transfer request.
  3. Submit a new transfer request.
