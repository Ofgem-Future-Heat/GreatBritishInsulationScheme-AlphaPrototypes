View all the approval requests your organisation has received. You can choose to accept or decline transfer requests by selecting "View details" under "Action".

What do the transfer statuses mean?
Status Explanation
Approval of transfer requested The transfer request is successfully raised but is not accepted by other energy supplier yet.
Request for approval being assessed The transfer is ready to be reviewed by Ofgem internal users.
Request for approval withdrawn The supplier who raised the transfer request has 'withdrawn' their request.
Request not supported The supplier who received the transfer request has 'Declined' the request.
Transfer not approved Transfer request has not been approved by Ofgem.
Transfer approved Transfer request has been approved by Ofgem.
